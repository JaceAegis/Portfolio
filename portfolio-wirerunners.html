Wirerunners

Wirerunners is an action, 2 player co-op, 3D game made in Unreal Engine.

Both players play as futuristic thieves trying to steal hidden data from a megacorp. Each player has to complete a minigame to hack the doors and progress. Once they got the information they want, they have to flee from the monsters sent for them

Key Features

  • Advanced Combat System
  • Complex AI Opponents
  • Dynamic Level Design
  • High-Fidelity Graphics
  • Multiplayer Support
  • Performance Optimization
